Event Details

Date: December 4th, 2012

Time: 7pm - 9pm

Location: Class of '49 Auditorium, Houston Hall (3417 Spruce Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104)

Cost: $5 admission, which includes your wristband and a raffle ticket.

RSVP: Let us know you're coming and a little about yourself and receive a free pass to Bally Fitness! RSVP »
